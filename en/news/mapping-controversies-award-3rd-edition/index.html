Mapping controversies Award – 3rd edition

published by Equipe FORCCAST on 03 Oct 2014

filed under Activities · Controversies · Events · Public speaking · International mapping controversies price · Novela

On Monday 13th October 2014, the festival of shared knowledge La Novela and the FORCCAST project, hosted by Bruno Latour and Dominique Boullier, carried on with the open presentation of methods and tools that allow us to find our way in the great issues of our time by attributing the Mapping controversies Award.

Six Mapping controversies works will be awarded among different schools from the Consortium Démoscience: Les Mines Paristech, Les Ponts ParisTech, Télécom ParisTech, Politecnico di Milano, the University of Neuchâtel and Sciences Po Paris.

A discussion on cyclone Xynthias presented by a working group of SPEAP (program of experimentation in arts and politics) will follow.

The day will end with a debate between the audience and the participants, so be ready for this rich and interactive experience and bring your questions!
